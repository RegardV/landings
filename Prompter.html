How to Print Your Prompt Cards

Follow these steps to ensure your prompt cards print correctly on A5 paper:

  1. Select A5 Paper: Use A5-sized paper (148mm x 210mm or 5.83in x 8.27in). If unavailable, cut A4 paper in half.
  2. Open Print Dialog: After generating cards, click the "Print Cards" button to open the print dialog (or press Ctrl+P / Cmd+P).
  3. Set Paper Size: In the print dialog, go to "Preferences" or "Properties" and select "A5" under paper size. If A5 is not listed, choose "Custom" and enter 148mm x 210mm.
  4. Adjust Layout: Set orientation to "Portrait" and margins to "None" or "Minimum" (5mm is applied automatically).
  5. Print Settings: Ensure "Print on Both Sides" is off and scaling is set to "100%" or "Actual Size" to avoid resizing.
  6. Preview and Print: Check the print preview to confirm one card per A5 page, filling the space with writing lines. Adjust if needed, then print.

Note: Printer settings vary by model. Consult your printer’s manual if you encounter issues with A5 printing.
