Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about creative coaching.

What exactly is creative coaching?

Creative coaching is a collaborative process where we work together to identify and overcome the obstacles in your creative practice. I help you develop strategies, build sustainable habits, and reconnect with your creative purpose. Unlike traditional therapy, creative coaching is goal-oriented and focused specifically on your creative work and process.

How do coaching sessions work?

Sessions are conducted via Zoom and last 60-90 minutes, depending on the package you choose. We'll discuss your current challenges, review your progress, and develop strategies for moving forward. I provide exercises and assignments between sessions to help you implement what we discuss. All sessions are recorded so you can review them later.

What types of creatives do you work with?

I work with a wide range of creative professionals and enthusiasts, including visual artists, writers, designers, musicians, performers, and entrepreneurs with creative businesses. My approach is adaptable to various creative disciplines, and I tailor my methods to your specific medium and challenges.

How long before I see results?

Many clients experience insights and small breakthroughs after just one session. However, sustainable change typically requires consistent work over time. The 4-session package often provides significant momentum, while the 3-month program allows for deeper transformation and integration of new creative habits.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

I'm committed to providing exceptional value. If after our first session you don't feel we're a good fit, I offer a full refund for any unused sessions. For the 3-month program, I offer a 50% refund if canceled within the first two weeks. This policy ensures you can try coaching with confidence.
